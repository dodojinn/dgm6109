How emotional impact relates to my meals, temperature, and hot meal preference

Hypothesis: Stronger emotional impact may relate to more meals per day, and external factors like temperature may also influence hot meal choices.

Each circle represents a day. The x-axis shows emotional impact (0-5), the y-axis shows meals per day (0-4), the circle size represents hot meal percentage, and the circle color represents temperature (cool → warm → hot). To reduce outliers, days with only one meal were removed using Array.filter().

This visualization helped me understand how different dataset properties can be layered together in a single scatterplot. I practiced combining color, size, and filtered subsets to reveal patterns more clearly. Using Array.filter() allowed me to remove one-meal days that acted as outliers, which made the overall relationship easier to read. I also learned how thoughtful color choices and consistent spacing can improve the clarity and mood of a visualization, helping the chart feel both readable and expressive.
